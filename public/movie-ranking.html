Eric's Top 3 Favorite Movies

My favorite movies of all time, in no particular order!

Shrek

This was the first movie I owned on VHS. (I know, I'm old.) I watched it over and over as a kid, and memorized almost the whole thing!

The Lost World of Jurassic Park

This is the second movie in the original trilogy. It featured the T-Rex the most out of the three movies, which is my favorite dinosaur!

2 Fast 2 Furious

This is the second movie in the Fast and Furious franchise. It introduced Roman Peirce and Tej Parker, which are two of my favorite characters!
